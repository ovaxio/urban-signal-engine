# ADR-004 — GTM strategy and commercial decisions
Date: 2026-03-10 | Status: Accepted | Source: Claude.ai GTM analysis (3 iterations)
## Decision
Primary: securite privee / evenementielle Lyon. Entry via paid one-shot rapport, not free pilot.
## Values (immutable until explicitly changed)
- **Entry**: rapport evenement **390 EUR HT** one-shot
- **Upgrade**: abonnement **490 EUR HT/mois** after 2-3 rapports
- **Secondary** (week 6+ only): logistique API **149 EUR/mois**
- **Pivot trigger**: 0 devis after 10 qualified secu conversations by week 4 -> conseil 800-1500 EUR
## Targets
- Securitas, SAMSIC Event, Goron, GL Events, Groupama Stadium, LDLC Arena, Halle Tony Garnier
- Decideur: Responsable Exploitation, Directeur Agence
## NO-GO segments
- Collectivites (AO 2-4 ans) | Retail (tension != footfall) | Assureurs (6-12 mois actuarial)
- Securite publique (accreditations) | Immobilier (12-24 mois collective decision)
## DO NOT
- Reopen NO-GO segments before primary validated. Start logistics before week 6.
- Offer free 30-day pilot (paid rapport IS the pilot). Add freemium tier.

Re-read when: pricing, segment evaluation, client feature request, landing page copy
